# 💰 ARBITRAGE FINDER - INVESTOR OVERVIEW

## What is This?

**Arbitrage Finder** is a fully functional, production-ready software platform that automatically identifies and analyzes profitable buy-and-sell opportunities across global e-commerce marketplaces.

**In Plain English:** 
The software finds products being sold on eBay that people are actively buying, then automatically searches global marketplaces (China, Japan) to find the same products cheaper. It calculates ALL costs (shipping, customs, fees) and tells you exactly how much profit you'd make.

## How It Works - 4 Step Process

### Step 1: Identify Demand ✅
- Scrapes eBay for product activity
- Measures: Sold listings, active  listings, actual selling prices
- Validates with Google Trends data
- **Only products with >50 demand score proceed**

### Step 2: Find Global Sources ✅
- Searches Taobao, 1688, Alibaba (Chinese suppliers)
- Searches Yahoo Auctions, Mercari (Japanese suppliers)
- Compares prices across all platforms
- **Must be significantly cheaper than eBay price**

### Step 3: Calculate True Cost ✅
- Item purchase price
- International shipping (EPacket, EMS, DHL)
- Service aggregator fees (Mulebuy, Superbuy, Buyee, Zenmarket)
- Import duties & customs
- eBay final value fees (12.9%)
- PayPal processing fees (2.9% + $0.30)
- **Result: Exact landed cost**

### Step 4: Identify Winners ✅
- Products with >30% profit margin
- High demand validation (80+ demand score)
- Ranked by overall opportunity score
- **Only viable products shown to user**
